My Story

I am a third-generation textile artist from Lagos, Nigeria. I learned this craft from my grandmother, who taught me that every pattern tells a story of our people, our traditions, and our resilience. Each piece I create is woven with natural fibers sourced from local communities, supporting sustainable practices and preserving traditional techniques passed down through generations.
