How Artsy Began

Artsy was born from a simple observation: across Africa, millions of skilled artisans create extraordinary handmade pieces, yet struggle to reach customers beyond their local markets. Meanwhile, collectors worldwide seek authentic, culturally significant crafts but have no trusted platform to find them.

In 2024, a team of women from Nigeria, Kenya, Botswana, Uganda, Algeria, and South Africa came together during the Women Techsters Fellowship to solve this problem. What started as a capstone project quickly grew into a mission-driven platform that now connects over 2,400 artisans with buyers in 120+ countries.
